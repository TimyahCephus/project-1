What Is Formula 1

Formula One is the highest class of international racing for open-wheel single-seater formula racing. The FIA Formula One World Championship has been one of the premier forms of racing around the world since its inaugural season in 1950.

How fast are they

The modern F1 car can reach speeds of roughly 220mph on average on straights.

Teams That Participate

There are 10 teams that compete in formula 1 currently. Those include RedBull, Ferrari, McLaren, Mercedes AMG, Aston Martin, Alpine, Alfa Romeo, AlphaTauri, Williams, Haas f1.
